WELCOME TO SPACE

There's a lot we don't know about our universe. Is there other life out there? Who can say? I'd like to think there are entire worlds and civilizations we can't even fathom. Have they created arbitrary social hierarchies like we have? Or are they smarter than that? What does their popculture look like? Do aliens make movies? Are their films anything like ours? One can only imagine.

This is a sort of imagined alien utopia. No laws, no wrongdoing, no needless suffering. The aliens don't need social constructs, oxygen, or any of that human junk. This galaxy is a party that never ends. Feel free to stay for a while. Enjoy the music!
